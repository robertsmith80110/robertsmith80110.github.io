5 Tips for Hassle-Free Car Shipping

Published on October 29, 2025 by Robert Smith

Shipping your car across states can feel stressful, but with the right preparation, it’s simple and safe. Whether you’re moving or buying a vehicle from another state, DIY Transport makes it easy.

1. Get Multiple Quotes

Always compare car shipping prices from different carriers. At DIY Transport, we provide transparent quotes with no hidden charges.

2. Check Insurance Coverage

Ensure your auto transporter has full insurance. This protects your vehicle during transit.

3. Prepare Your Vehicle

Clean your car, remove valuables, and document existing damage with photos before pickup.

4. Understand Delivery Windows

Shipping times can vary due to distance and weather. Stay in touch with your assigned driver for real-time updates.

5. Inspect Upon Delivery

When your car arrives, inspect it carefully and note any issues before signing off.
